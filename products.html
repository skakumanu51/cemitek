HReze (Workforce & Talent Management System)

HReze is a user-centric HCM with all the standard features at half the cost. If you are a sponsoring company or have sponsored employees, we have an immigration module that comes included with the base offering. The application was built from the ground up. It was designed and built by people familiar with all aspects of the employment process. Our core being software engineering, we truly appreciate the desire to minimize the amount of time spent on administrative tasks. This has resulted in a streamlined intuitive user experience. Improved visualization of the data that is important to you with the ability to drill down to the details as needed. Finally, enter the data once and see it reflected everywhere and in different contexts as warranted.

Features

Employee Management

Employee Profile Workflows, Client/Project management, Resource allocation, Payroll

Timesheets

Submit your timesheets with ease and the updated timesheets can be easily pushed to backend to generate invoices which can be sent to the vendors.

Employee self-administration

Standard profile management, Immigration and Work-authorization modules, time and attendance, and paystubs

Salary

Salary information can be viewed and payslips can be downloaded through the app. 

Leaves

Effective employee leave management process where he/she can manage his/her leaves. He/she can apply leave easily through the app and can easily monitor the leave balance and history of leave applied. 

Mobile-first

Application is available both in iOS and Android Platforms.
